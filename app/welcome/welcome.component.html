Welcome!

This is a tool for taking live statistics for sports or esports. When you're ready, check our the "Add" and "Events" tabs. When an event happens, you will add the specifications in the "Add" tab and hit submit. Then you will see the event has been added in your event tab. Once you are done adding events, click the submit button and you will be taken to a game summary report that you can save or take a screenshot of. To restart with a new game, just refresh the page.
