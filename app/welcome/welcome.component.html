Welcome!

This is a tool for taking live statistics for sports or esports. When you're ready, check our the "Add" and "Events" tabs. When an event happens, you will add the specifications in the "Add" tab and hit submit. Then you will see the event has been added in your event tab. Once you are done adding events, go to the "Summary" page and click the "Get Summary" button to get a game summary report that you can save or take a screenshot of. To restart with a new game, just refresh the page.


IMPORTANT
Make sure you enter each name, game, and stat the exact same!
Also, here are the types of events that work with this tool:

Rocket League:

  • Shot
  • Save
  • Goal

Valorant:
  • Kill
  • Death
  • Spike Plant

Hockey:
  • Shot
  • Save
  • Goal

Basketball:
  • 2 Points
  • 3 Points
  • Foul
